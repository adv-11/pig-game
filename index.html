NOTE : IF PLAYING ON PHONE SHIFT TO HORIZONTAL MODE A. Two players take turns to play the game.

B. If the player rolls a:
   1: They lose all their current points, and it's the other player's turn.
   2 to 6: The rolled number is added to their current score, and they have the option to roll again (see next step) or hold their turn.

C. The player can choose to:
  1. Roll Again: If they choose to roll again and roll a 1, they lose all their current points, and it's the other player's turn.
  2. Hold: If they choose to hold, their current score is added to their total score, and it becomes the other player's turn.

D. First player to 100 wins !
